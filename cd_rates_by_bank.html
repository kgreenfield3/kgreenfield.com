CD Rates by Bank: Full Case Study Coming Soon!


Author & Stakeholders
Kyrsten G. (UX Researcher), Gabe R. (PM), and Justin S. (Designer)

Summary
The CD Rates by Bank template was developed in order to drive traffic to the site and increase bank monetization. Working aside Design and Product, I conducted all research initiatives associated with this project. User research was completed in the beginning of the project to better understand our user base and their goals, needs, motivations, etc. through the use of surveys and industry research. Competitor and general usability testing were done to uncover the strengths and pain points of both our design and the designs of our competitors. All usability testing was conducted using Usertesting.com. During my research, I was able to uncover two key insights that helped in from our design strategy:
  • The older audience, one of our key user groups, views CDs as a low risk, long term investment tool.
  • A comparative experience is most effective after the primary product is introduced.

Results & Next Steps
Performance data is not yet known as the page is not live. However, during the development of this page, the Design / UX team did uncover a potential strategy related to our monetization units. This strategy uses insights from user testing to better design and place monetization units on the page.
