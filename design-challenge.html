DESIGN CHALLENGE

Designing One Prompt at a Time

Completing 30+ design prompts for digitial and print products

  • Project Timeline: 4 weeks

  • My Role: UX/UI Designer

  • Tools: Figma

Prompt 1: Sign-Up Form

A simple sign-up form for a fake dog walking service called “Rover’s Route”, that requires an account to schedule walks for your 4-legged friend. The form includes inputs for Name, Email, and Password and gives users the ability to sign up using their Gmail address.

Photo by charlesdeluvio on Unsplash

Prompt 2: Credit Card Checkout

A credit card checkout module for Home Vista, a fake Airbnb-type service. This module displays the rental property's location, dates, and total cost, providing users with a clear summary. Users have the option to complete the checkout process using either a credit card or debit card.

Photo by Luca Bravo on Unsplash

Prompt 3: Above the Fold (Landing Page)

The above-the-fold experience for the website of Stampede, Pittsburgh's most electrifying country band. This experience prominently features links to their about page, schedule, YouTube channel, and social media platforms. On mobile devices, the hero image cycles through individual pictures of the band, introducing the new Stampede.

Stay tuned for the full case study, coming soon.

Prompt 7: Countdown (+ App Icon)

A period app that provides users with a countdown to their next menstrual cycle. The app features a calendar for tracking and logging relevant data. Additionally, it includes an app icon with a pink color scheme and blood-red cherries, representing its unique branding.
