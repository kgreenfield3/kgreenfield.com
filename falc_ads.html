Marketing Ads

The following ads are my first offical attempt at design ad creatives. These ads were created based on insights I gathered through user research of SmartAsset's Paycheck Calculator. The research showed that the users of the Paycheck Calculator were much younger than the user base of our other priority pages with a majority falling between 18 - 34 years of age. This discovery led us to a more humorous and dynamic set of ad creatives.

I used Sketch and Bannersnack to create the ads, which are currently being tested on SmartAsset.com.
