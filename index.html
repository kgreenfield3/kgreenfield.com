Research & Design Systems
Research & Design Systems

As the UX lead at SmartAsset, one of my primary objectives was to formalize and optimize the research and design process. I took charge of overseeing and promoting design and research standards, guidelines, and processes, ensuring a seamless and effective UX practice throughout the organization. Under my guidance, we established a comprehensive component library encompassing style guides, custom iconography, typography, colors, and fundamental UI elements. This cohesive resource enhanced our productivity and maintained consistency across all design projects. Additionally, we fine-tuned our device breakpoints and integrated an 8-point grid system into our design practice, further refining our approach. On the research front, we focused on templatizing our narrative-based deliverables that could be easily utilized (and referenced) by anyone within the organization. By standardizing the documentation of insights and findings related to our core audience and product usability, we fostered a shared understanding and facilitated collaboration across teams.
