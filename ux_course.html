UX Course

The UX Course was developed by me and the Creative Director to introduce design and UX principles, examples, and ways to them in-house to our Product team. The course occured on a weekly basis from Aug - Dec 2018.

  • Goal: Create a more informed, empathic team by sharing Design / UX resources.
  • Methods: The UX Course was loosely based on Stanford d.school’s virtual crash course on Design Thinking. Each week, we’d focus on one step of the Design Thinking process by providing definitions, examples, projects that would have benefitted from this procedure, and how we could implement it at SmartAsset.
  • Insights:A combination of internal and external examples helped everyone grasp not only the various concepts, but the value of Design Thinking as a whole.
  • Results: Every member of the Product team has a better understanding of the Design Thinking process and are showing signs of being more empathetic towards our users. Due to the success of this pilot course, the organization is also more open to the idea of internal education.
